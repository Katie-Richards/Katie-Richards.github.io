Our Services

Learning Materials

At Space Explorers we provide a wide range of learning materials both instore and online. Whether you are only just starting out, or you want to expand your vast knowledge of the universe, we have the resources for you.

Telescopes

We offer a wide range of telescopes, from amature to professional, including attachments and services, both instore and online. Get yourself equipped with the best so you can enjoy your stargazing.

Astrophotography

All of your Astrophotography needs met instore and online, including tip, tricks, and equipment. Make your passion of photographing the universe easier today.

Sky Watching Meetups

At Space Explorers we offer sky watching meetups, where astronomy enthusists can find groups just like them and go out to look at the stars.
